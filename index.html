Attack Strategies We Implement

Penetration Testing

Simulating external and internal attacks to test the strength of your security systems. Using advanced techniques to mimic tactics used by hackers, such as phishing, brute force, and SQL injection. Providing detailed reports on discovered vulnerabilities and actionable recommendations to fix them.

Read Team assessments

IDeploying a dedicated team of cybersecurity experts to act as adversaries, testing your organization's ability to detect and respond to attacks. Simulating multi-vector attacks, such as blending social engineering with network exploitation.

Social Engineering Campaigns

Testing employee awareness by simulating phishing emails, baiting attacks, or fake customer service calls. Analyzing employee behavior to identify gaps in awareness and training needs.

Threat Hunting Simulations

Proactively searching for hidden threats within your network by mimicking advanced persistent threats (APTs). Utilizing data analysis, log reviews, and behavior analytics to detect stealthy adversaries.

Zero-Day Exploit Simulations

Evaluating how well your system can handle unknown vulnerabilities by replicating zero-day attack scenarios. Assessing your organization's response times and contingency measures.
